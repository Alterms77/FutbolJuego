# FutbolJuego – Development Roadmap

## Phase 1 – MVP (Months 1-3)
- Core match simulation (Poisson xG model)
- Single Premier League with 4 teams
- Squad management & tactics screen
- Basic transfer market (free agents only)
- Firebase Auth + save/load
- Android build on Play Store internal track

## Phase 2 – Alpha (Months 4-6)
- Full 20-team league with fixture generation
- Cup competition
- Transfer window (buy/sell between AI clubs)
- Training & injury systems
- Economy & infrastructure upgrades
- AI opponents (Easy/Medium/Hard)
- Leaderboard & daily rewards
- iOS TestFlight build

## Phase 3 – Beta (Months 7-9)
- Expert AI difficulty & tactical adaptation
- Match Predictor Tool (Monte Carlo)
- Live events & weekly challenges
- Season Pass monetisation
- Rewarded ads integration
- Localisation: ES, PT, DE, FR, IT, JA
- Performance optimisation (object pooling, LOD)
- Soft launch (selected markets)

## Phase 4 – Launch (Months 10-12)
- Google Play & App Store global launch
- Continental competition (unlock at manager L10)
- Push notifications (match reminders, daily reward)
- Marketing campaign & influencer partnerships
- Post-launch: biweekly live events, seasonal updates
