# FutbolJuego Monetisation Strategy

## Premium Currency – FutCoins
**Sources:** Daily login, weekly challenges, achievements, rewarded ads, IAP  
**Uses:** Kit cosmetics, extra scout slot (non-simulation), skip 4h fatigue timer

## IAP Tiers
| Pack            | FutCoins | Price  |
|-----------------|----------|--------|
| Starter Pack    | 200      | $0.99  |
| Value Pack      | 1 100    | $4.99  |
| Season Pass/mo  | 500+perks| $6.99  |
| Mega Bundle     | 3 000    | $12.99 |

## Season Pass (monthly)
- Daily FutCoin bonus (+15/day)
- Exclusive club kit skin
- 1 extra free-agent scout slot
- Priority match simulation speed

## Rewarded Ads (opt-in only)
- Double XP for next match (1× per day)
- Recover 20 fatigue from one player
- Reveal hidden player potential for 24 h

## Non-P2W Principles
- No simulation stat boosts purchasable
- All gameplay content earnable f2p
- Premium = cosmetics + convenience only

## Ad Strategy
- Interstitials: max 1 per session, never mid-match
- Rewarded ads: always optional, never forced
- Banner: none (poor UX for management games)
